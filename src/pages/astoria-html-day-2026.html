✳️ HTML Day 2026 in Astoria Park ✳️

a freewrite under the Hell Gate Bridge — write some html by hand, by the water.

On Saturday, August 8th, 2026, people all over the world will gather to celebrate HTML Day: a day to meet up IRL and write and learn HTML together. I'm hosting one of those gatherings — a freewrite — in Astoria Park, in the green shade of the Hell Gate Bridge, where Queens meets the East River.

A freewrite is exactly what it sounds like. Bring a laptop, bring an idea, and write a little HTML the way our forefathers & foremothers once did — no framework, no build step, no JavaScript required. Hand-code a poem, a homepage, a love letter, a list of birds you can see from the park. Then push it to a server and, boom, you're on the web. That's the whole web0 dream.

No experience needed. If you've never written a tag in your life, come anyway — I'll get you from blank file to live page before the sun gets too low over the river.
