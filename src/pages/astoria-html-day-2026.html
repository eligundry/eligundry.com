✳️ HTML Day 2026 ✳️

a freewrite in Astoria Park, under the Hell Gate Bridge

Saturday, August 8th · 2–5 PM South Hill, Astoria Park · map ↗

Once a year, people all over the world gather IRL to celebrate HTML Day — a day to sit down together and write HTML by hand. I'm hosting one of those gatherings, a freewrite, on the grassy South Hill of Astoria Park, in the green shade of the Hell Gate Bridge.

Bring a laptop and write a little HTML the way our forefathers & foremothers once did — no framework, no build step, no JavaScript required. A poem, a homepage, a love letter to the river. I'll be working on some poetry, so feel free to do the same. Never written a tag in your life? Come anyway — I'll get you from blank file to live page before the sun gets low.

Good to know

  • Bring a blanket — we'll be on the grass.
  • I'm bringing a sandwich; pack a snack or a picnic of your own.
  • My kid will be hanging with us for part of the afternoon, so it's a kid-friendly crowd.
  • Poetry welcome and encouraged — HTML optional but lovely.
  • Charge your battery; there are no outlets in the park.
